JOEL CARTER

Artist | Musician | Producer

Presents His Debut Album:

MOOD MUSIC VOL. 1

Born Into Music

Growing up in Texas, I group up in a with 4 siblings who could sing or play musical instruments. My Dad was also a musician, and my mom was a singer. Music was kinda our thing. We even had a family band called...wait for it..."The Carter Children". We sang a churchs throughout Texas, with varying memberships. I believe that our largest audience was about 5,000 people. So as now you can see that music has always been a friend to me. Today as a husband and father of 2 girls, I'm continuing the tradition of creating an environment where music interest are encouraged.

The Birth of a Musician

When I had the chance to join the orchestra in Junior High, I jumped at the opportunity. Still, I couldn't choose just any instrument. I had to pick the biggest and baddest of them all... the double bass. I may have been slightly influenced by the fact that both of my older brothers, were also Bassists. Over the years I've picked up a few more instruments along the way. I've spent my adult life playing for others, but this album was about expressing what I hear. I hope this album finds you well, and helps you find your groove...no matter you MOOD.

Chasing a Dream

This album is all of my musical dreams, come to life. Finally it's a chance to play the music that I like. Now even though it's all original music, I guarantee there's something on here to pique your interest. So make room on your playlist for some new favorites. Let's Vibe!
